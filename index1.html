Instructions

  1. Adding Data: If you want to add data, click on "Add Data" in the navigation menu.
  2. Viewing Stored Data: To view stored data, scroll down to the "Stored Data" section on the page. You'll find a table that lists your saved data, including website names, usernames, and passwords.
  3. Copying Data: If you need to copy a piece of data from the table, click the "Copy" button in the "Action" column for the respective row.
  4. Contact Information: For inquiries or to connect with the website owner, visit the "Contact" section. You'll find links to the owner's LinkedIn, GitHub, Instagram, and other social profiles.
